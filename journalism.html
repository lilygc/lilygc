150+ articles, commentaries, features, and academic pieces published across national and local outlets — from wire service dispatches to Oxford University Press. Spanning women veterans, domestic violence, poverty, data, public health, and more.

Featured piece

"Where Are the Male Victims at Lackland?" — San Antonio Express-News, January 2013

At the height of the Lackland Air Force Base sexual assault scandal, coverage focused exclusively on female victims. Drawing on data showing that male service members experience military sexual trauma at significant rates — and are far less likely to report — this op-ed made the case for broadening the conversation.

The day after it appeared, four male victims came forward.
