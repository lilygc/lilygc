Consulting & AI

A emerging area of focus: applying AI tools — particularly Claude — to research, nonprofit work, and public scholarship. Certified by Anthropic in both foundational AI use and AI applications for the nonprofit sector.

Orientation

The intersection of AI and mission-driven work is where I'm headed. Having spent years translating complex data and research into forms that reach real audiences, AI tools offer a natural extension of that work — accelerating research synthesis, improving data analysis workflows, and making sophisticated outputs accessible to nonprofits and community organizations that lack large technical teams.

I am available for consulting on AI integration for nonprofits, research organizations, and public health entities — particularly around responsible use, workflow design, and translating AI capabilities into practical community impact.
