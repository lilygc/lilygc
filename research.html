Research & Publications

Research

Survey research is a particular specialty — designing instruments, fielding community-wide studies, analyzing results, and translating findings into policy-relevant outputs. The work focuses on vulnerable populations: women veterans, survivors of domestic violence, low-income communities, and students experiencing homelessness.
