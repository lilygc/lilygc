Research & Publications

Research

TEDx San Antonio, 2019

Research became a natural outgrowth of journalism. Ask good questions of the right sources — and lots of them — and you can develop formalized, actionable observations about what's working, what's not, and what paths forward might look like. Survey research became a particular specialty: designing instruments, fielding community-wide studies, analyzing results, and translating findings into outputs that reach both academic audiences and the people the data describes.
